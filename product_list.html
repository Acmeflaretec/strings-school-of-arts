COURSES AND ADMISSION CRITERIA

Indian culture based course based on the syllabus of Sikkim Skill University & Kerala kalamadalam Every year, examinations on Carnatic Vocal, Hindustani Sangeeth, Sitar, Veena, Violin, Mridangam, Panchvadhyam Otanthullal, Kathakali,Bharadhanatyam, Kathak Mohiniyattam.

Western courses are based on the syllabuses of University of West London College of Music Examination & Rock School Of London. Every year, examinations on Piano, Violin, Guitar, Cello, Jaz Drums, Western vocal Music Production and theory of music will be conducted by eminent musicians who come from LCME&RSL Certificates to successful candidates will be offend directly by the LOME & RSL.
